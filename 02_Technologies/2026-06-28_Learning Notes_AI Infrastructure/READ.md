# What I Newly Learned Today 28-06-2026

## 1. FC-BGA May Remain as a Large Panel Until the Final Stages

* The size of the AMRs appears to remain almost unchanged throughout the production process.
* The black carriers contain large substrate panels.
* Large carriers are also used around the VIA MECH equipment.
* Although I cannot yet conclude that the panels remain intact until the very end of the manufacturing process, I confirmed that panel-level transportation continues at least through the later production stages.

---

## 2. VIA MECH Is a PCB Processing Equipment Manufacturer

* VIA MECH (Via Mechanics) is a Japanese manufacturer.
* It is a globally recognized manufacturer of PCB drilling and laser processing equipment.
* The CO₂ LASER equipment is highly likely to be used for microvia processing in FC-BGA substrate manufacturing.

## 6. CUDA and Blackwell Are Completely Different Concepts

To organize the concepts:

* CUDA = Software platform
* Blackwell = GPU architecture

Even after the Blackwell generation is eventually replaced, CUDA may continue to remain as the software foundation.

---

## 7. NVIDIA Is Not the Only Path

Companies such as Google are developing custom ASICs together with Broadcom.

In other words,

AI chips are **not limited to NVIDIA GPUs**.

ASICs are

Companies such as

Technology continues to evolve.
