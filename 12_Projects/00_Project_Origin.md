# Where Does AI Wealth Go?

## Why This Project Started

This project did not begin with IPOs.

It began with a simple question:

**What is interest?**

While studying Japanese real estate and interest rates, I realized that the discussion eventually led to AI companies, Compute investment, IPOs, and liquidity.

This raised a new question.

AI companies invest enormous amounts of capital into Compute before generating profits.

When that capital eventually becomes liquid through IPOs or secondary sales, where does the wealth go?

Does it return to the next generation of AI companies?

Does it move into real estate?

Does it become long-term capital preservation?

The objective is not to study IPOs themselves.

The objective is to understand how capital created in the AI economy flows after liquidity.

This project has just begun.

It will evolve as new observations, interviews, and evidence accumulate.
