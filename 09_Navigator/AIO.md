# AIO

## Current Definition

AIO (AI Optimization) refers to the process of becoming discoverable, interpretable, and selectable by AI systems.

---

## Why This Concept Was Needed

As AI increasingly mediates information access, traditional SEO no longer fully explains how visibility is allocated.

A broader framework was needed.

---

## Personal Origin

Before studying AI, I spent years working with SEO.

SEO was not simply about search engines.

It was about understanding how visibility is allocated.

Who gets seen.

Who gets ignored.

Who appears first.

And why.

At the time, I thought this was a problem limited to Google.

I was wrong.

## Key Realization

The problem was never search.

The problem was allocation.

Search engines allocate attention.

Recommendation systems allocate visibility.

AI systems allocate opportunity.

The mechanism remained similar while the environment changed.

---

## Discarded Explanations

### SEO is only marketing

Rejected.

Visibility allocation exists far beyond marketing.

### AI is only automation

Rejected.

AI increasingly functions as an allocation mechanism.

### Search and recommendation are separate systems

Rejected.

Both determine what becomes visible.
