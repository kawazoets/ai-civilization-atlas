# AI-Scored Society

## Current Definition

AI-Scored Society refers to a social environment in which visibility, trust, responsibility, and opportunity are increasingly allocated through AI-mediated evaluation systems.

---

## Why This Concept Was Needed

What problem was not adequately explained by existing frameworks?

- Human evaluation appeared increasingly inconsistent.
- Market outcomes often diverged from effort and performance.
- Recommendation systems were influencing visibility.
- Traditional social theories did not fully explain these changes.
